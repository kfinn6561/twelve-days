# Image Sourcing Guide for "Twelve Days of Christmas" Gifts

This guide provides direct links to search for free, high-quality images for each of the 12 gifts from "The Twelve Days of Christmas" carol.

## Free Image Resources

All images from these sites are free to use, even for commercial purposes, typically without attribution required:

- **[Unsplash](https://unsplash.com)** - Free high-resolution photos, no attribution required
- **[Pixabay](https://pixabay.com)** - Free images under Pixabay License
- **[Pexels](https://pexels.com)** - Free stock photos
- **[Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org)** - Public domain images

## Download Instructions

1. Click on the links above for the gift you're sourcing
2. Browse the search results and select an image you like
3. Download the image (preferably high resolution)
4. Rename the file to match the format: `day-1.png`, `day-2.png`, etc.
5. Place the image in: `/public/assets/images/`
6. Replace the existing placeholder file

## Image Requirements

- **Format:** PNG or JPG (PNG preferred for transparency)
- **Size:** Minimum 500x500px (will be scaled down for web)
- **License:** Ensure the image is free for commercial use
- **Quality:** Clear, well-lit images work best

## Tips for Best Results

- Look for images with simple, clean backgrounds
- Avoid overly busy or cluttered images
- Try to maintain consistent style across all 12 images
- Higher resolution is better (you can always scale down)
- Square or near-square aspect ratios work best for the scattered layout
